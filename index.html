About Heritage Fest

Celebrating Our Rich Cultural Heritage

Heritage Fest 2025 is a grand cultural celebration organized by the Hare Krishna Movement, Bhilai. This festival aims to preserve and promote our rich Indian cultural heritage through various art forms and competitions.

Join us in this magnificent journey of cultural exploration where participants showcase their talents in Dance, Music, Literature, Theatre, Drawing, and Decoration. Experience the divine essence of our traditions while competing for exciting prizes.

Our mission is to create a platform where young minds can connect with their roots and express their creativity through traditional and contemporary art forms.
